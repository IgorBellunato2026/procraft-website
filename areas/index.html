How We Work Across Locations

Local familiarity.
Studio-grade work.

Whether the project is a flat off Sloane Street or a country property in Berkshire, the process is the same. Igor visits the property in person, the brief is talked through on site, and the quote is drawn up against the actual scope — not against a generic template.

For every postcode, we know what is involved: which boroughs require party-wall agreements, which estates require freeholder consent, which properties are listed. That knowledge is built in to how we plan the work.

The crew travels with the project. The standard of finish does not move with the postcode.
