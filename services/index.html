Why Commission ProCraft

Single-studio accountability.
No broken chain.

Most refurbishments fail in the hand-offs: the joiner blames the tiler, the tiler blames the plasterer, the project manager promises to look into it. Nobody takes responsibility, and the client absorbs the cost.

ProCraft is structured to remove that friction. Every stage of the work is led by the founders and delivered by a crew we know personally. If anything is not right, there is one person to call — and the problem is ours to fix.

That accountability is the reason we can guarantee our workmanship, commit to transparent pricing, and still hand over only when you are completely satisfied.
