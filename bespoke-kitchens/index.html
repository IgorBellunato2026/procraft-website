Why Master Carpenters

A kitchen is, fundamentally,
a piece of joinery.

Most kitchen companies are designers first and tradespeople second. The cabinetry is sub-contracted, the worktops are sub-contracted, and the installation goes to whoever is available. The result is a kitchen that looks fine in the photographs and falls apart at the seams.

ProCraft Refurbishment was founded by two master carpenters — Igor and Leandro. We approach every kitchen the way a cabinetmaker approaches a piece of bespoke furniture: with reverence for the materials, attention to every joint, and intolerance for shortcuts. We design the cabinetry. We build the cabinetry. We install the cabinetry. The work is never delegated to a different crew with different standards.

This is why the difference is in the corners. Look at how doors meet. Look at how the worktop joins the wall. Look at the alignment of the handles across a run of drawers. These are the details that distinguish a bespoke kitchen from a fitted one — and they are the details we obsess over.

Materials & Finishes

The materials we work with —
and why they matter.

A kitchen is only as good as its materials. We are happy to discuss every option, but these are the materials and details that consistently produce the kitchens we are proud to put our name to.

Worktops
Marble (Carrara, Calacatta, Statuario), quartzite (Taj Mahal, Cristallo), granite, sintered stone (Dekton, Neolith) and solid surface (Corian). We template on site, slab-match where joints are unavoidable, and finish edges by hand for the profile your design calls for.
Cabinetry construction
Solid timber face frames where the design supports it, 18mm moisture-resistant carcasses, dovetailed drawer boxes, full-extension soft-close runners, and concealed Blum or equivalent hinges. Cabinets are built in our workshop and delivered as finished components.
Door styles
Shaker (in-frame and lay-on), handleless (J-pull and recessed channel), slab-front, beaded, and bespoke profiles to match period property detailing. Painted in your specified colour from Farrow & Ball, Little Greene, Paint & Paper Library or any commercial system.
Hardware
Brushed brass, antique brass, polished nickel, brushed steel and bespoke leather pulls. Sourced from Armac Martin, Joseph Giles, Corston and other premium British manufacturers.
Appliances
Wolf, Sub-Zero, Gaggenau, Miele, Siemens, Bora and Quooker. We will install client-specified appliances or, where preferred, source through trade channels and pass the saving on.
Sinks & taps
Fireclay (Shaws, Belfast), polished and brushed steel (Franke, BLANCO), and bespoke options. Brassware from Perrin & Rowe, Lefroy Brooks and Drummonds for traditional schemes; Vola, Dornbracht and CEA for contemporary.
Lighting
Recessed downlights, under-cabinet LED strips with diffusers, pendants over islands, and concealed plinth lighting. All on dimmer circuits, scene-controllable where the brief calls for it.
