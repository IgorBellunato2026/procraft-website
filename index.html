Who We Are

Craftsmanship
Is Our Standard

ProCraft Refurbishment Ltd is a London-based team of skilled tradespeople with a singular commitment: exceptional quality on every project, without exception.

Founded by two master carpenters, we bring a craftsman's eye to every detail — from the first consultation to the final finish. We don't cut corners, we don't rush, and we don't compromise.

Our clients are discerning homeowners and property investors who understand that a great refurbishment isn't a cost — it's an investment that adds lasting value to their property.
