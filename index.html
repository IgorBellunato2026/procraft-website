Why ProCraft

A great refurbishment is not a cost.
It is an investment that lasts.

Most refurbishment companies are project managers first. They sub-contract the joinery, sub-contract the tiling, sub-contract the finishes — and the client pays the price in delays, surprises and substandard work.

ProCraft was founded by two master carpenters: Igor and Leandro. Every project begins with a property visit and ends with a final walkthrough we do not leave until you are completely satisfied. The work is led by craftsmen, not co-ordinated by spreadsheets — and the difference is in the corners, the joints, and the alignment of every detail.

We do not compete on price. We compete on quality, precision and professionalism. That is the whole proposition.

How We Work

Four steps.
No surprises.

Our process is built for clarity. You always know what is happening, when it is happening, and what it costs.

Step 01

Free Consultation

Igor visits the property, listens to the brief and assesses the scope. No charge, no obligation.

Step 02

Detailed Quote

You receive a fully itemised quote — line by line, no hidden costs, no upsells, no ambiguity.

Step 03

Expert Execution

The work is led personally by Igor and Leandro with our trusted crew. Regular updates throughout.

Step 04

Final Walkthrough

We hand over only when you are completely satisfied. Snags addressed promptly. Workmanship guaranteed.
