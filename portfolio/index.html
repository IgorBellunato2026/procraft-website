Featured Project

Green Kitchen
Transformation, Chelsea.

A complete bespoke kitchen refurbishment for a Chelsea townhouse. Hand-built cabinetry in deep green with brushed brass hardware, marble worktops with waterfall edge, and a fully integrated appliance suite.

The brief: a kitchen that worked as the social heart of the house and stood up to daily family use without apology. The challenge: working around the period detailing of the existing space and matching the cornicing back precisely.

Build time: 8 weeks. Delivered on schedule, on budget, and to a finish that has prompted three referrals from neighbours since handover.

Featured Project

Pink Bathroom Suite,
Kensington.

A luxury en-suite bathroom in a Kensington apartment. Walls in soft blush microcement, herringbone marble flooring, brushed gold brassware throughout, and a bespoke vanity built in solid oak with a single piece of stone.

The brief was for a bathroom that felt like a hotel suite without losing the warmth of a private home. The work involved relocating the soil stack — coordinated with the freeholder and the building's managing agent — and a full re-board for acoustic compliance.

Build time: 5 weeks. Photographed for the studio's Instagram feed where it remains one of the most-shared projects to date.

Featured Project

Herringbone Floor,
Ascot.

A full ground-floor flooring installation in a substantial Ascot property. Wide-plank European oak laid in a traditional herringbone pattern across reception rooms, hallway and orangery — approximately 180 square metres.

The work involved comprehensive sub-floor preparation, acclimatisation of the timber over two weeks, and installation around three sets of bi-fold doors and a feature staircase. Hand-finished and oiled on site for a soft, lived-in matte finish.

Build time: 3 weeks excluding acclimatisation. The flooring transformed the entire ground floor of the property and was specified to last a generation.
