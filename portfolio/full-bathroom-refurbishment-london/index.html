The Project

Full bathroom
refurbishment, London.

A complete bathroom refurbishment delivered end-to-end for a London property. The brief was for a bathroom that read like a private suite — restrained palette, considered detailing, and finishes that would still feel correct ten years from now. The work involved stripping the existing bathroom back to brick, tanking the wet area to BS 8102 standard, and rebuilding it as a single coherent room.

The new specification combines marble tiling laid by hand from the spine outwards, a bespoke timber vanity with single-piece stone top, brushed gold brassware throughout, frameless glass shower templated on site, and underfloor heating below the new floor build-up. Plumbing, electrics and ventilation were re-routed for the new layout; the existing soil stack was retained but reworked behind the new joinery to keep the visible plumbing concealed.

Delivered by the ProCraft crew from first measure to final snag, signed off personally before handover. The room continues to be in daily use and reads exactly as the brief described: a private spa inside a London home.
