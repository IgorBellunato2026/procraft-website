The Project

Master Bathroom Suite,
London.

A master en-suite bathroom in a London property. Bespoke vanity in solid timber, brushed gold brassware throughout, frameless walk-in shower templated on site, and herringbone marble tiling laid by hand from the spine outwards.

The brief was for a bathroom that read like a private spa — restrained palette, considered detailing, and finishes that would still feel correct ten years from now. The work involved relocating the soil stack, tanking the wet area to BS 8102 standard, and a full re-board for acoustic compliance with the building.

End-to-end delivery from strip-out to snag, by the same crew throughout. Signed off personally on handover.
