The Project

Master bathroom
suite, London.

A modern master bathroom for a London property. The walls are tiled to dado height in cream stone-look large-format tile and painted above; the floor is the same stone-look tile, full-room. The composition is calm and contemporary, designed to read consistently in every light condition through the day.

The walk-in shower sits behind a brass-framed glass screen, lined inside in herringbone marble-look tile and fitted with a brushed brass rain head and handheld set. Opposite, a fluted black wall-hung vanity carries a single-piece white basin with a brushed brass tap and a back-lit mirror. A brushed brass heated towel rail is wall-mounted between the vanity and the shower; an oak door reads warm against the brass.

Approximately 4 weeks on site. The work involved stripping the bathroom back to brick, tanking the wet area to BS 8102 standard, rerouting plumbing for the new layout and a full re-board. Delivered by the ProCraft crew end-to-end, signed off personally on completion.
