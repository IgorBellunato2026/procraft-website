The Project

Family kitchen
refurbishment, Ascot.

A compact family kitchen refurbishment for an Ascot property. The brief was a hard-working U-shaped layout with a peninsula that returns into the room. Wall cabinets are Shaker in light grey; base cabinets are Shaker in olive green; both runs use the same brass pull as a quiet through-line. The worktop is a single white stone surface running across the full peninsula and rear runs.

Integrated appliances sit on the left run, with a black hob set into the peninsula. A statement stainless steel cylindrical hood is suspended from the ceiling above the hob and works as the room's visual anchor. A stainless steel one-and-a-half bowl sink with chrome mixer sits below the rear window. The doorway to the right opens onto the staircase. The floor is a clean wood-effect plank.

Approximately 4 weeks on site. Delivered by the ProCraft crew end-to-end, from strip-out through cabinetry install to the final snag.
