The Project

Family Kitchen
Refurbishment, London.

A bespoke family kitchen refurbishment for a London period home. Hand-built cabinetry in a soft neutral palette, marble worktop on a central island, integrated appliance suite, and herringbone hardwood flooring laid throughout the open ground-floor space.

The brief: a kitchen that worked as the practical centre of a busy household, with enough storage to absorb everyday family life and enough composure to hold its own when the room was used for entertaining. The work involved a structural alteration to the rear of the ground floor and the reinstatement of period detail elsewhere.

Delivered end-to-end by the ProCraft crew, from first measure through to the final snag. The kitchen continues to be in daily family use and remains photographically intact.
