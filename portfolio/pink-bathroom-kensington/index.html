The Project

Pink Bathroom Suite,
Kensington.

A luxury en-suite bathroom in a Kensington apartment. Walls in soft blush microcement, herringbone marble flooring, brushed gold brassware throughout, and a bespoke vanity built in solid oak with a single piece of stone.

The brief was for a bathroom that felt like a hotel suite without losing the warmth of a private home. The work involved relocating the soil stack — coordinated with the freeholder and the building’s managing agent — and a full re-board for acoustic compliance.

Build time: 5 weeks. Photographed for the studio’s Instagram feed where it remains one of the most-shared projects to date.
