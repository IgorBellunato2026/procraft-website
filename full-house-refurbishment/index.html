Why It Matters

A whole-house refurbishment
is a programme, not a project.

Refurbishing a house is fundamentally different from refurbishing a room. The trades have to be sequenced precisely. Long-lead items have to be specified months in advance. Cabinetry has to fit alongside structural alterations done six weeks earlier by a different team. Decisions made in week three about wiring constrain decisions made in week eighteen about lighting.

Most contractors handle this badly. They work in silos. They sub-contract critical trades to whoever is cheapest that month. They cost-cut when something goes wrong, and the homeowner discovers the consequences a year after handover. ProCraft was built specifically to do this differently. We treat a whole-house refurbishment as a single design-and-build commission — with a single Gantt programme, a single point of contact, and a single guarantee on every aspect of the work.

Igor leads every project from initial site visit to final walkthrough. Leandro coordinates execution on the ground. The trusted crew we use for larger jobs has worked with us for years — there are no surprises about who turns up on Monday morning.

Programme & Project Management

How we manage
a whole-house programme.

A full refurbishment runs for months. The difference between a smooth project and a stressful one is the rigour of the management. This is how we work.

Master Gantt programme
A detailed week-by-week schedule produced at the quotation stage, covering every trade, every long-lead item and every dependency. You see exactly what is happening on site every week, and what is on the critical path.
Single point of contact
Igor is your point of contact for the duration of the project. Decisions, change orders, queries and issues all route through him — no chasing multiple sub-contractors, no conflicting answers.
Weekly progress updates
A short written progress report and a photograph package every Friday. We flag risks early, propose mitigations, and keep you informed without flooding your inbox.
Itemised payment schedule
Payments tied to project milestones, not arbitrary calendar dates. You pay against work completed and verified, never against promises. Standard 20% VAT applies to all sums and is shown separately on every invoice.
Change order discipline
Changes mid-build are quoted and approved in writing before work proceeds. We never proceed on a verbal "yes" — protects you from surprises and us from misunderstanding.
Compliance & certification
All certifications — electrical (NICEIC/NAPIT), gas (Gas Safe), building control completion, FENSA windows, structural sign-off — provided as a single handover pack at completion.
12-month workmanship guarantee
All workmanship guaranteed for a minimum of 12 months. Manufacturer warranties on appliances, hardware, brassware and materials passed to you on handover. Snagging addressed promptly.
